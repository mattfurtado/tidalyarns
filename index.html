Local Wool, Locally Spun
Naturally Dyed at Home

I begin by carefully selecting the most beautiful fleeces and yarns from New England sheep farms. These fleeces are either handspun or gently processed at small New England mills. I hand dye all of the fiber at home, often using plants grown in my garden. These yarns are full of subtleties and variations that become beautifully apparent as you knit.
