Naturally Dyed & Handspun Yarns

I begin by selecting the most beautiful fleeces and yarns from New England sheep farms. One piece at a time I hand dye the fiber, often using what grows in my own backyard. The yarn is then baked in the sun and steamed on the stove for long-lasting color. Each skein is unique and can be combined with others to create lovely and interesting finished pieces. Enjoy watching the color patterns unfold as you knit.
